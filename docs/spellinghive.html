SPELLING HIVE
How to play

Spelling Hive is a tournament-based variation of the NYTimes' Spelling Bee.

To play, create a name and click Buzz on in. Once inside, you can join either team 1 or team 2, upon which you'll be thrown into the current round.

Each round is 120 (or whatever, figure out a time) seconds long.

To win, your team must submit the greatest number of words that are made up of the letters given, greater than three letters long, less than 15 and contain the center letter.

Have fun!
